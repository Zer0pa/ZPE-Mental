# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Use this repository in good faith. Be direct, evidence-led, and respectful. Challenge claims with reproductions and concrete citations, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- bad-faith evidence disputes
- scope-widening claims that ignore the committed proof surface
- posting private material, secrets, or undisclosed third-party data

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or close threads that violate these standards.
